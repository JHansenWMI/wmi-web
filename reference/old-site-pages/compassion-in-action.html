The Dorcas Fund

The Vision of the Dorcas Women and Children Fund


We believe that every life has immeasurable value. No child should be forgotten, no widow should be left to carry her burdens alone, and no family should lose hope because of tragedy, poverty, rejection, or circumstances beyond their control.

The Dorcas Women and Children Fund exists to reach those the world often overlooks, the orphan whose parents have died, the child abandoned or rejected because of a disability, the widow, the single mother, the single father, the struggling family, and the forgotten communities where help seldom comes. We intentionally go to the overlooked orphanages, the neglected community centers, and the forgotten places, because we believe they matter deeply to God.

Through the generosity of compassionate hearts, we provide food, clothing, school fees, beds, mattresses, books, toys, sanitary supplies, soap, and other essential needs. Every gift is more than practical help, it is a declaration that someone sees them, someone believes in them, and that God has not forgotten them.

As God opens doors, we carry His love from nation to nation, serving the forgotten in Kenya, Israel, Colombia, and now Malawi.

We may not be able to reach everyone, but for every child embraced, every widow comforted, every family strengthened, and every forgotten soul reminded that they are loved, a life is changed and hope lives again.

Founder Dr. Adalia Hansen
