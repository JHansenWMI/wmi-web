January 2026 - Canada - Sheriffs Rally - General Mike Flynn

3/27/2026
Pastoral Articles, Mission Trips, North America, New World Order, United States of America

On January 8, 2026, we met in Surrey, Canada in several meetings to discuss the churches responsibility in addressing the threat of a Communist takeover of Canada. We also discussed how we can work together in different nations including, Malaysia and Hong Kong. Vee Sitoh’s daughter, who is a medical doctor, could assist my wife in treating widows and orphans in Kenya and other nations such as the Philippines. 

After the meetings in Surrey, we met with the Supeyo’s in Langley, Canada to continue the discussion on the potential Communist takeover of Canada and to define the responsibilities of true Apostolic Leaders in responding spiritually and proactively, placing Canada under Jesus Christ. We also discussed other nations where we can work collaborate such as Israel.

After our meetings concluded in Langley, we traveled to Puyallup, Washington, to take part in a major conference organized by the American Police Officers Alliance “Save Our Sheriffs Rally”, in partnership with the Constitutional Sheriffs and Peace Officers Association (CSPOA). The event was held at the Experience Christian Church pastored by Dennis Cummings. I was personally invited to attend the VIP meeting where I met with the different Sheriffs, Senators, Congressmen and other leaders. General Mike Flynn was the featured speaker, and I had the opportunity to spend time with him, including a special breakfast meeting, with my son present. At one point, General Flynn kindly watched Michael while I got my breakfast, which spoke volumes about his character. I was very touched and impressed. 

This conference was organized in response to efforts in Washington State to replace elected Sheriffs, even pursuing legal action against those who actively fight crime. It was a tremendous conference. Since then, Washington State has passed a concerning bill that could enable greater control over the public by limiting their rights to retain the sheriffs they elect, thereby endangering their freedom. I invite you to join me in leading a national repentance.  Join Eagles Saving Nations now at worldministries.org.
