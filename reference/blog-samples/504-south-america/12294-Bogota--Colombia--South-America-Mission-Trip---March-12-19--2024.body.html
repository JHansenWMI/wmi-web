March 19, 2024, The team at the Bogota International Airport heading home

My family & I with Vance Miller flew out of SeaTac airport in Seattle, WA on March 12, 2024 and arrived in Bogota, Colombia airport on the same day. We flew out of Bogota, Colombia on March 19, 2024 and arrived at SeaTac airport in Seattle, WA after 11pm that evening.

Upon arrival in Bogota, I was met by a delegation who told me that what I had prophesied five years earlier at a conference where I was invited to speak to 23,000 apostles literally came to pass. At those meetings in 2019, I read the Prophecy to Colombia that God had given me on June 18, 2003 at 1259 hrs and further warned, "If the Apostles etc., of Colombia did not lead a Great Awakening/National Repentance, Colombia would be led by a Communist president, crime would go out of control, the economy would collapse and the Communist government would start to take away the church's freedom." The welcoming delegation told me they now have a Communist president and government, crime is out of control, the economy crashed and their freedoms are being taken away. The leaders and church in Bogota, Colombia, were and are, desperate and we had the best meetings that I have seen in forty years. One lady, who was bleeding from cancer and on the way to the hospital, begged to be taken to our meetings instead and was instantly healed. PTL

In all of my meetings, privately and publicly, I warned of the dangers of the New World Order that are trying to topple the sovereignty of every nation. I warned of the need for the believers to arise with the power of the Holy Spirit through Eagles Saving Nations (ESN), to stop the agenda of evil men in every nation that are working with the world elite to implement a cashless society. Their goal is for every person to be marked/chipped in order to be able to buy or sell. I organized an Apostolic Team to set up meetings in the stadiums for national repentance and for the power of Pentecost to fall on the believers attending those meetings, so they could go forth with power and authority instead of fear and intimidation, bringing transformation to Colombia.
