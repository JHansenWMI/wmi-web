WASHINGTON D.C. JANUARY 20-23, 2025

4/14/2025
Pastoral Articles, Mission Trips, Africa, Asia, North America, Europe, South America, Israel, New World Order, United States of America

What a tremendous trip we had to Washington D.C. We were invited to President Trump’s inauguration on January 20th and the Congressional Israel Allies Caucus in the 119th Congress on January 21st. Many attended including members of the Knesset, U.S. Senate and House of Representatives, both Republican and Democrat who support Israel, as well as dignitaries from around the world and some church leaders. I am a member of the Knesset Christian Allies Caucus and I also support the Israel Allies Foundation. The morning before the 119th Congress, I interviewed Dr. Augie David on television. On January 22nd, we prayed in front of the White House and visited the rotunda in the Capitol. I also did a television program in front of the Congress Building. On January 23rd, I did five other television programs in front of both the United States Supreme Court and the U.S. Capitol addressing abortion being allowed in some states, if they chose to permit it. I asked for the President to reconsider this choice allowed by the states; as it is one of the four sins that brings judgment on the nation as explained in my book, THE SCIENCE OF JUDGMENT. If killing a baby in the mother’s womb is murder in some states, it should be wrong in every state, as it was before Roe vs Wade. Four sins bring judgment on a nation: idolatry, immorality, killing the innocent, and dividing the land of Israel. I also touched on homosexuality, which was formerly a crime in America. Our nation used to understand the Bible and the judgment that comes to that nation when homosexuality is legalized.

I hope you enjoyed the photos and reading about these mission trips. I do need your continual support to go to the nations and be a representative/ambassador of the gospel of Jesus Christ. The bride of Christ is being persecuted in many nations and all nations are being coerced, in an attempt to have them join the New World Order. In every nation I am calling for a national repentance against the four sins that bring judgment on a nation. We need a national repentance in America, and a change in our laws so that sins such as idolatry, immorality, killing the innocent and dividing the land of Israel are not condoned and legalized in the U.S.A. Please support me at worldministries.org. God bless you.
