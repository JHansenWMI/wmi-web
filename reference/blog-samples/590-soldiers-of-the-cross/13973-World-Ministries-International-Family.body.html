World Ministries International Family

5/14/2026
Pastoral Articles, Soldiers of the Cross

Dear World Ministries International Family,

We give thanks to God for His covering over our recent mission to Kenya. Through your prayers, the trip was not only successful but deeply impactful. We held multiple gatherings, including a powerful women’s conference, Sunday services, and private ministry sessions. In every setting, the presence of God was undeniable, bringing deliverance, healing, and breakthrough to many lives.

We witnessed doors opening beyond what we expected, even into other nations. Truly, your prayers carried us and we are grateful for your unwavering spiritual support.

As we look ahead, we are preparing for missions in Malawi and Tanzania in due time this year. We humbly ask you to continue standing with us in prayer for divine provision, for partners, and for laborers willing to go into the field. 

Through our Dorcas ministry, we are believing God to extend practical love to communities in need. Our vision includes supporting widows and orphans, providing clothing, food, and basic necessities such as sanitary pads, etc., and empowering women by helping them start small businesses. We desire to bring both spiritual and tangible transformation to these villages and communities.

We ask that you pray with us for helpers, resources, and hearts willing to join this mission. May God connect us with those who will hold our hands in this work, so that together we may serve and uplift those in need.

Thank you for standing with us. Your prayers are not in vain.

With gratitude and faith, Dr. Adalia Hansen

 

Dear Saint,

  1. We thank God for His provisions or we could not travel the nations.
  2. We need to travel to Tanzania and Malawi soon, as we have been requested to go to both. The needs in both of these African countries are enormous. As soon as we receive $10,000 for each of these countries, we will buy the tickets and go. We will go to whichever country we receive the finances for first, followed by the second country. 
  3. Zambia is on the horizon as God is opening the doors and the Holy Spirit is confirming in 2026, we will also labor there for the Lord. If we receive a donation of $30,000 for these three countries, we will immediately purchase our tickets and put together the itinerary for these nations. 
  4. Time is racing by and the New World Order is already strategizing to control these nations. Only the church can stop it with proper leadership leading a National Repentance. Help me to meet with the leadership of both, church and government, to come under the authority of Jesus Christ and lead a National Repentance. 
  5. Thank you for your prayers and your financial response to these requests as I know people do pray and one or more of you give to these critical requests and special needs. 
  6. I desperately need thousands of additional intercessors. Please join our team we call The Soldiers of the Cross and help us pray our way to victories. Respond today to this email article, or call (360) 629-5248 or sign up on our web site: worldministries.org. 

Blessings, Dr. Jonathan Hansen
