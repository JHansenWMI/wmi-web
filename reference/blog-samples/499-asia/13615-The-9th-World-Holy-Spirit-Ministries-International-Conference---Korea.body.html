The 9th World Holy Spirit Ministries International Conference - Korea

1/6/2026
Pastoral Articles, Mission Trips, Asia

On the 29th of September 2025, seven members of my esteemed WMI team embarked on a flight from Sea Tac, WA., to Incheon, South Korea, arriving at precisely 4:30 pm on the following day. I selected five of my dedicated staff to join me as speakers at The 9th World Holy Spirit Ministry International Conference in Buchun City, South Korea. The conference spanned from October 1st to the 3rd, featuring approximately 50 international speakers and worship singers who flew into minister. The majority of speakers conducted breakout sessions, while a select few addressed the General Session.

Upon our arrival, once all the participants had gathered, we boarded a team bus that transported us to a restaurant for dinner, before finally checking into our hotel around 11pm. We commenced our days by leaving the hotel promptly at 8 am on Wednesday mornings for breakfast at the church, a routine we followed daily. The conference officially commenced at 9 am each day and continued until approximately 9 to 10 pm every night. Mornings were dedicated to worship and the general session until noon, followed by lunch, and then breakout sessions from 2 to 5 pm, allowing attendees to choose their preferred class. Dinner was served from 5 to 7 pm, and the evening services were filled with worship, singers, dancers, and a keynote speaker. Despite the long days, the experience was immensely gratifying, with a strong focus on the Holy Spirit and an abundance of worship during each service, rejuvenating us with the power of the Holy Spirit.

Saturday, October 4th, was a well-deserved day off, during which we engaged in a tour. On Sunday, October 5th, I delivered a speech at the Gloria Church led by Senior Pastor Young-Kwang Shin, with my WMI team, the Hawaii team, and my wife participating. Later that evening, we flew back to Seattle, while the Hawaii team returned to Honolulu. All the other speakers and singers departed from Korea on Saturday evening, as attested by the Korean Church below. I urge you to read this testimony and extend your support as | journey across nations to forewarn, prepare, and safeguard Christ's bride.

To the Korean Church: I bring you greetings from Drs. Jonathan and Adalia Hansen. The need for the believer to be filled and led daily by the Holy Spirit is greater now than ever before. I warned prophetically at the beginning of 2025 that this year is going to be a very prophetic dangerous year where the believer needs to be very sensitive and flexible to the leading of the Holy Spirit. I started traveling each month to different locations. On January 14, 2025 we were at Mar-a-Lago, Florida with the Jerusalem Prayer Breakfast (JPB) to pray for President Donald Trump. One week later on January 21, 2025 we attended the 119th US Congress for the Israel Allies Foundation as l am a member of the Knesset Christian Allies Caucus (KCAC). In Washington D.C., | visited with various political leaders from the Senate and House of Representatives of America as well as with leaders from around the world.

In February and March we were in Kenya, East Africa, and in April in Norway. In May in Africa again. In June, we were on our way to Israel to attend the KCAC when Israel bombed Iran's Nuclear Sites and Israeli Air Space and their airport was shut down and our airplane from New York to Tel Aviv was cancelled.

Other nations we visited in 2025 were Liberia, Malawi, Columbia, Israel, the Philippines and here in South Korea. In each nation I addressed the need for a National Repentance and warned about the tyranny that is sweeping the nations, taking away the freedoms of individuals. I warned that their plan is to destroy the Old Order and bring us all into the New World Order (NWO).

The only power that can stop the evil plans in each nation is the baptism of the Holy Spirit as Jesus commanded his disciples in Acts 2: 1-4 to make disciples in every nation through His power!

I have clearly warned if the church doesn't arise and do more than pray, the church will lose its freedom. We were commanded by the Lord when He ascended to make disciples of every nation. This Holy Spirit Conference is critical.

Blessings, Rev. Dr. Jonathan Hansen
Founder & President of World Ministries International & Eagles Saving Nations
B.A. Liberal Arts, B.A.Th., M. Th., M. Div., D. Min., D. Th., and Honorary D. Div.
