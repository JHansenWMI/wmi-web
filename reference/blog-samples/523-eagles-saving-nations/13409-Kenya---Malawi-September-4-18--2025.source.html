Kenya & Malawi September 4-18, 2025

9/28/2025
Pastoral Articles, Mission Trips, Africa, New World Order, Eagles Saving Nations

On September 4th, 2025 we flew from SeaTac International Airport to France and then to Nairobi, Kenya, arriving Sept 5th. We were met at the airport by one of my WMI staff Paul, as well as Apostle Joe Mwaniki. We were driven to our lodging residence where Arch Bishop Harrison Nganga and his wife met us. We enjoyed some fellowship and prayed together for the salvation of Kenya.

On Sept 6th, my wife, son and I visited with Bishop Geoffrey and Elizabeth Njuguna at their house before we met for dinner with the former Vice President, Stephen Kilonzo Musyoka at his home. In all of my meetings, I discussed the things pertaining to the Kingdom of God and our responsibilities to heal, protect and save a nation.

On Sunday, Sept 7th I spoke at Bishop Thomas Muthee’s, Word of Faith Church with my wife, Dr. Adalia, ministering to many people at the altar.

On Monday Sept 8th, we were driven to Jomo Kenyatta Int’l. airport by Dr. Mike Hyodo, my WMI staff representative in Kenya, where my family and I flew to Malawi, by invitation, to be with the President, Lazarus Chakwera.

Upon arrival in Malawi, we were met on the tarmac, put into a black vehicle, and driven to the airport lounge where our luggage and passports were brought to us. We never saw the inside of the airport terminal but were driven straight to the President’s house and then taken to our lodging.

While in Malawi, Sept 8-15, I had a chauffeur, security agent and vehicle which accompanied us everywhere. We spoke to Apostolic Leaders as well as a church. We spent time with the President. Please look at the close of this article to see what the Lord said, through me, to the President.

Sept 15-17, we were back in Nairobi, Kenya where I met with Bishop Geoffrey Njuguna, discussing the situation in Kenya and what the church must do to save the nation. My wife and I also prayed for a young lady and her mother. We also prayed for Bishop Michael Wafula who had suffered a serious health issue. God was healing him. We also caught up with Mike and Cheryl Freese; missionaries to Kenya and flew from Kenya to Amsterdam together.

These two weeks in Kenya and Malawi were tremendous as far as ministering to nations. The freedoms of both nations hang in the balance, depending on the actions of the true Bride of Christ. Both nations need a National Repentance to lift the curses and usher in God's Blessing and Protection.

The Church must unite from tribalism and corruption to change Kenya.

In Malawi, President Lazarus Chakwera was God’s choice, but Satan, through evil spirits and men, hacked into the voting system. Men and officials were bribed and the true results were manipulated, giving the election to his opponent. In every nation, God’s choice for President doesn’t usually win because God cannot force His will on man. The Bible tells us that Satan is in charge of this world and he rules through evil men. Unless the church in nations become true ambassadors and obey the Great Commission to make disciples throughout the nations; to rule, reign, occupy, be salt and take dominion, evil men will rule instead of the righteous. God allows this and He uses these evil leaders to chastise the righteous until they rise up in holiness and to put in men of God to rule their nation.

The church in Malawi now needs to do what President Trump did when they stole the second election from him. He continued to speak the truth as truth takes away deception and prayer brings conviction. The Republicans, with the help of the church, united the church more, prayed more and ensured the election was more honest as they focused on correcting the hacking of votes and other irregularities.

My prayer is that the church will rise up and do what I previously mentioned so that in 2030 President Lazarus will be elected again, that God can use him to usher in five years of prosperity; that he will be surrounded by loyal, honest staff; that he will deal with the corruption and enemies of Malawi who are trying to serve themselves and take the nation over. May he have God’s favor, blessings and anointing; that his next five years be a turning point in Malawi because of his leadership; that new destinies will manifest! Cloud will depart and the sea will divide and President Lazarus Chakwera will lead Malawi to safety and prosperity. May God bless President Lazarus Chakwera and his family. Jonathan Hansen
