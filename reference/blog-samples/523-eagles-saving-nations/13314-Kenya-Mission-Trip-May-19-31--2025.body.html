Kenya Mission Trip May 19-31, 2025

8/12/2025
Pastoral Articles, Mission Trips, Africa, Eagles Saving Nations

We flew from Sea Tac International Airport on May 19, 2025 and arrived at our lodging in Nairobi, Kenya at 0200hrs, on May 21, 2025. This mission trip was for leadership both, political and church, including international leaders. Leadership is responsible for the blessing or deterioration of a nation. I counseled and prayed with numerous people and warned about my prophecy I gave President Moi for Kenya in 1992 which is still coming to pass; and they are on the last point. May 22, 2025 we attended 'The Night to Honor Israel’s Independence' and then throughout the next two weeks, we met daily with different leaders where I always counsel and pray with them. I was also the special guest of the former Vice President Stephen Kalonzo Musyoka at a large celebration gathering and prayed for him.
