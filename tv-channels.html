KAZQ TV-32 Coverage Information

Over-the-Air Coverage
KAZQ TV-32 signal covers approximately 1.1 million people throughout Central and North Central New Mexico.  Communities covered include:  Albuquerque, Belen, Bosque Farms, Bernalillo, Cedar Crest, Corrales, Edgewood, Isleta Pueblo, Los Chaves, Los Lunas, Moriarty, Rio Rancho, Santa Domingo Pueblo, San Felipe Pueblo, Sandia Park, Santa Fe, Tome, Placitas, Paradise Hills, Taylor Ranch, and Tijeras.

Satellite Coverage
KAZQ TV-32 is also available via the Dish Network on satellite 110, channel 8817 and on DIRECTV on satellite 119, channel 32, throughout most of New Mexico, portions of Southern Colorado, and into North Apache County of Eastern Arizona.  This increases KAZQ’s potential reach approximately 1.8 million people.  Communities covered via Dish Network include:  Albuquerque, NM, Belen, NM, Carlsbad, NM, Cedar Ridge, AZ, Dunton, CO, Durango, CO, Farmington, NM, Gallup, NM, Grants, NM, Los Lunas, NM, Marble Canyon Arizona, Pleasante View, CO, Portales, NM, Roswell, NM, Santa Fe, NM, Silver City, NM, Second Mesa, AZ, Shiprock, Arizona, Taos, NM, Tuba City, AZ, and Tucumcari, NM.

Cable Coverage
