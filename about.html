About WMI

Our Mission

To faithfully speak to the nations of the world, proclaiming the kingdom of God; giving warning to political and church leaders, the church and society of the signs of the return of Jesus Christ.  We are sounding the alarm for the nations to turn from sin and to draw near to Jesus Christ.  Our purpose is to influence and transform the nations whereby enabling peace, blessings and prosperity to come to the people.  We are endeavoring to meet emotional, physical, psychological and spiritual needs through the love of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

  • To reach 100% of the world with the gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • To change the destiny of nations through reconciliation with God.
  • To bring truth and restoration to the body of Christ and to set people free from bondage.
  • To strengthen and unite followers of Jesus Christ into an army to do battle against Satan and the anti-Christ spirit.
  • 2,000 Intercessors in every country in which we minister.

Our Commission 

The mission and vision are being realized by the following means:

Crusade Meetings

Dr. Hansen ministers under the anointing of God to the nations, meeting with political and church leaders such as presidents, prime ministers, bishops, and pastors. He speaks in crusade and revival meetings, leads seminars for pastors, business persons, and medical professionals.  He also ministers apostolically to bishops and pastors worldwide.

Television

Our state-of-the-art video studio produces the daily television program WARNING, which is on over 205 cable and 29 prime-time television stations in the USA and other countries.

Website

Our website is updated daily and the WARNING radio programs are available on demand 24 hours a day, seven days a week. A comprehensive overview of the ministry is available, including prophecies to each nation, current news in prophecy, teaching articles, our statement of faith and much more.

Radio

Dr. Hansen is heard throughout the USA daily on the radio program WARNING. He also speaks for one hour weekly on short wave radio which reaches every nation in every continent.  He is interviewed frequently as a guest on radio stations throughout the world and has produced thousands of programs.

Bible College - World Ministries International School of Theology

We are committed to bringing sound, bible-based, Christian education to the individual and the local church. We have partnered with a strong Christian University to help establish local campuses and/or offer correspondence courses for individuals with the purpose of providing Christian education for effective practical ministry.

Studies are designed to train Christians and leaders to be filled with the Holy Spirit and to walk in the authority, anointing and the power of God. We are challenging students to yield their will in perfect obedience to Jesus Christ, so that they can become "flames of fire"

The financial investment is a mere fraction of what is required to attend a residential college.

Benevolence

Help us to help others. World Ministries International offers mission opportunities to serve people in needy areas. We provide relief aid in the form of food, clothes, bibles, horses, livestock, wood, etc.

We work with teams of doctors, dentists, and nurses, to provide medical care to the poor. We have built dental clinics in Kenya, Romania, Russia, El Salvador, Dominican Republic, and Jamaica. We minister to orphans, widows, refugees, the disabled, and the poor by giving relief aid, bibles, food, and clothing.

If you are interested in joining one of our mission teams or contributing to feed and serve the poor, please contact us today at (360) 629-5248.
