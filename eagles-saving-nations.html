Millions all over the world are aware, or are now becoming aware, of the urgent need to get involved locally, nationally, and internationally! Politicians and those in positions of power have become corrupt, insensitive, and are ignoring those who entrusted them to legislate the will of the people.

It is time to repent and return to what makes nations great! We need strong and honorable men and women who can discern right from wrong, good and evil, and who are governed by a deep conviction and commitment from our Creator.

The Vision and Mission is to wake up Christians of the seriousness of the hour and hold revival meetings across the world. To fill stadiums, conferences, and churches focused on training, strategy, exhortation, worship, preaching; allowing the glory of God to manifest with people being baptized in the Holy Ghost and others constantly overflowing with the Holy Spirit. We want them to leave these meetings encouraged, refreshed and strengthened with power and authority to accomplish confronting the evil forces that are trying to enslave God's people and their nation. We want to be true ambassadors of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.
