Dr. Hansens Bio

Dr. Jonathan Hansen is the Founder and President of World Ministries International (WMI), a non-denominational organization and is a fourth generation Christian, a fourth generation ordained minister and a third generation missionary. He ministers under the anointing of God to the nations and meets with political and church leaders such as presidents, prime ministers, bishops and pastors. He speaks in crusade and revival meetings, leads seminars for pastors, business people, medical professionals and counsels hundreds of bishops and pastors worldwide.

Education

Dr. Hansen currently holds the following degrees: Diploma in Ministerial Studies, Bachelors of Arts in General Studies (B. A.), Bachelors of Theology (B. Th.), Masters of Theology (M. Th.), Masters of Divinity (M. Div.), Doctorate of Ministry (D. Min.), Doctorate of Theology (D. Th.) and Honorary Doctorate of Divinity (D. Div.) and is currently ordained with the Independent Assemblies. Universities, colleges and seminaries where Dr. Hansen studied are: Vanguard University (Costa Mesa, CA), Central Bible College (Springfield, MO), Berean College (Springfield, MO), Eastern Washington University, (Cheney, WA), Asia Theological Center for Evangelism and Missions (Singapore), Nairobi International School of Theology (Kenya), Christian Life School of Theology (Columbus, GA) and Beacon Institute of Ministry (Columbus, GA).

Organizations

Apostolically, Dr. Hansen has trained people for ministry wherever he has traveled or pastored, sending them out to the nations. He also provides apostolic leadership to many different pastors throughout the United States, as well as other countries. Dr. Hansen is Chancellor of World Ministries International School of Theology, training people to become "...a flame of fire" according to Hebrews 1:7. Besides leading WMI, he served on both the Missions Committee and Board of Directors for Beacon University from 2006-2007.  In 2008, he began serving on the Missions Committee and Board of Directors for Christian Life Educators Network, where he is still serving. He is also the Bishop for Christian Life Educators Network (CLEN) over the nation of Kenya. Dr. Hansen is a member of the Apostolic Council of Transformational Servants-Leaders (ACTS), Columbus, GA and is the District Overseer for ACTS Network in Washington State. Dr. Hansen also serves as Apostle at Large globally for ACTS. Dr. Hansen is a member of the Allied Leaders for Middle East Peace (ALFMEP), Yakima, WA. In 2010, Dr. Hansen became a member of theInternational Coalition of Apostles (ICA), Dallas, TX. In 2012, Dr. Hansen was appointed as an Apostle at Large for the International Coalition of Apostles which is now called the International Coalition of Apostolic Leaders (ICAL). In 2013, Dr. Hansen was promoted to the board of the International Coalition of Apostolic Leaders.

On June 28, 2019, Dr. Hansen received an ordination as a Missionary Chaplain in Cali, Colombia by General Gral Wilian Equador of Chaplain Action Breaking Frontiers International Inc., with the rank of General. This is the highest rank and an extreme honor.

Dr. Hansen has studied and worked in the following fields: military, insurance, law enforcement, psychology, general studies, theology, ministerial studies and pastoral leadership. Dr. Hansen has graduated from the following military, state and law enforcement training institutions and police academies where he has earned the following certificates: Law Enforcement Specialist and Air Force Ground Defense from The United States Air Force Military Training Center (Lackland AFB, TX); Insurance Agent's License from The State of Washington, Insurance Commissioner; Basic Law Enforcement from The Washington State Criminal Justice Training Commission; Breathalyzer Operation from The Washington State Patrol Academy; Officer Survival from The Washington State Criminal Justice Training Commission; Basic SWAT Training from The Spokane County Sheriff and Sniper Training, Special Weapons and Tactics as well as Advanced Special Weapons and Tactics from The Washington State Criminal Justice Training Commission.

Besides America, Dr. Hansen has lived in Asia and Africa during his pastoral ministry. He has traveled the world over many times in ministry to the Bride of Christ. He is currently traveling to countries where God has given him a prophetic word for that particular nation and/or leader within that nation (president, prime minister, etc.). He speaks to thousands in crusades and conventions and to millions through radio, short wave and television.

Dr. Hansen served as a youth pastor before entering the United States Air Force, during which time he married his late wife Jeannie and they have four daughters; Tamara, Talitha, Kareen and Melita. The youngest is adopted; a miracle child whose throat was slashed and left for dead on a street in East Africa (see The Healing of Melita Hansen ). Later, Dr. Hansen served for seven years as Deputy Sheriff for Spokane County, Washington and during this time, he also served on their S.W.A.T. team. For three years, he presided as President of the Fellowship of Christian Peace Officers.

Dr. Hansen has served as a senior pastor, raising up pastors and sending them to nations, as well as serving as an interim pastor for three other churches. While in Africa, he spent three years as a presbyter for the Kenya Assemblies of God. He also served three years on the Spiritual Ministry Council (Executive Council) of the Uganda Assemblies of God (UAOG) and was their General Overseer for a season.

The Calling and Commission

Early in 1985, Dr. Hansen received his first vision from the Lord, which totally changed the direction of his life and ministry. In this vision, he saw himself traveling to the nations and speaking to the masses. He was shown the condition of the church and discerned that the majority of leadership were responsible for that sad condition. He became intuitively aware that God was grieved and angry over how the Shepherds had abused and raped His Bride. The Lord told him that many of the churches throughout the world had followed the example of their leaders, who are now trusting in themselves and the value system of the world more than they are trusting in God. He saw himself traveling throughout the nations of the world, exhorting the church to repent and prepare their lives for the return of Jesus Christ.

Returning from the vision, Dr. Hansen received an incredible, intense burden for the church in America and around the world. Within months, he resigned his position with the Sheriff's Department, terminated his business interests, resigned the Presidency of the Fellowship of Christian Peace Officers for Spokane County, terminated his work as a marriage counselor, put his house up for sale and started preparing for the responsibility of helping to restore and heal the church. In August of 1985, he and his wife traveled to Singapore in obedience to the vision and commission God had given him. There he served the Lord on the pastoral staff of Calvary Charismatic Centre (CCC), a Singapore Assemblies of God Church, founded and pastored by Rev. Rick Seaward. In Singapore, he served as one of the pastors, as well as Director over their short-term missionary program (Gideonites), which sends teams to plant churches. That year, he helped to train 90 church planting missionaries, which were then sent to three different countries where they established churches. While serving as the Senior Pastor of CCC, Nairobi, Kenya, he also served CCC Singapore for six years as the Assistant Regional Missionary Overseer for Africa. During this time, he also spoke non-denominationally throughout the world with a message of righteousness and holiness.

In early 1989, the Lord spoke to Dr. Hansen through a dream. In the dream, God showed him the events that the church was about to go through prior to Jesus' return. The revelation left him with an indescribable feeling of urgency and awe. For over one year, he kept the revelation of what God had shown him in his heart, only sharing it occasionally with a few churches. Finally in July 1990, God told him to record, in writing, the chain of events that he saw in the vision and the dream, to warn the church exactly where we are in history, the condition of the church, why God is not pleased with the church and what the church was about to go through. He felt compelled by the Holy Spirit that the time had come to warn the church of her condition and prepare her for what God was going to allow her to go through to purify and prepare her to meet her Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

WMI was birthed by the Holy Spirit with an objective to warn leaders of nations of the second coming of Jesus Christ and the plagues/judgments that are coming upon those people and nations that reject Jesus Christ as Savior, according to the Scriptures.

A Few Examples

* In 1992, Dr. Hansen warned Kenya that God is going to judge the nation. A spirit of rebellion followed by fighting, drought followed by famine and a spirit of Islam sweeping the nation, followed by riots. Finally, if the Christians of Kenya, especially the pastors, do not repent of their hypocrisy and develop a relationship with God, then the Christians will lose their religious freedom.

* Seven months prior to President Mobutu's fall, Dr. Hansen prophesied over public radio in East Africa that if President Mobutu failed to repent and if the people of Zaire failed to repent, President Mobutu would be overthrown, his government would collapse and the nation would fall. President Mobutu was chased from office, the government did collapse and the nation did fall. Today, Zaire is called The Republic of Congo.

* Between 1994 and 1996, Dr. Hansen prophesied the Asian Stock Market Crash, naming the countries.

* In April 1997, Dr. Hansen publicly prophesied that President Moi of Kenya would be re-elected in December of 1997. At that time, President Moi's popularity was approximately 20% and re-election looked impossible, but it did happen.

* In March 1998, Dr. Hansen spoke in the capitals of Burundi, Rwanda and Kenya. He also met privately with the President of the Parliament of Rwanda, Joseph Sebarenzi Kabuye. They met in his office at Parliament for an hour and a half regarding the spiritual condition of the nation and its destiny and gave conditions for peace which they followed and there has been peace to this day.

* In March 2001, Dr. Hansen flew to New York and spoke in public meetings, as well as on television, warning of the impending 9/11 attack, stating that skyscrapers would fall, blood would flow down faces and the people would still not repent.

* Dr. Hansen flew to the city of New Orleans three times before the hurricane Katrina hit the area, warning that judgment was coming due to the wickedness taking place in that city.

Other Changes

In August 1995, the Lord moved Dr. Hansen and his family back to America where he began to prepare to warn the church and the people of the United States of God's judgment upon them and why. The Lord gave him a release to begin on September 29, 1997, when he shared the warning and prophecies on a Seattle radio station. In 1998, he was released to begin sharing with the people at large. Dr. Hansen still travels extensively internationally, sharing the warnings of God and what the nations can do to hold back God's judgment.

In May 2016, Dr. Hansen's first wife, Jeannie, went home to be with the Lord. God brought another woman into his life to help him fulfill the commission he was given by the Lord. On August 25, 2018, he married Dorcas Adalia, a Kenyan lady. God confirmed His will for Dr. Hansen with three pages from different people with visions, dreams and revelations identifying whom he was to marry. On July 16, 2019, Adalia gave birth to their son, Michael Jonathan Jedidiah Hansen.
